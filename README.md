## Task Management

### Personal To-Do

- [Todoist](https://todoist.com) – Fast cross-platform to-do app with natural language dates, recurring tasks, filters, and labels.

- [Microsoft To Do](https://to-do.office.com) – Simple task lists with Microsoft account sync, reminders, and My Day planning.

- [Things](https://culturedcode.com/things) – Premium Apple-ecosystem task manager built for GTD-style clarity and daily planning.

- [TickTick](https://ticktick.com) – To-do + calendar + habit tracking with Pomodoro, tags, priorities, and flexible views.

- [Any.do](https://www.any.do) – Task management with daily planning, reminders, and simple collaboration for small teams.

- [Remember The Milk](https://www.rememberthemilk.com) – Classic task app with powerful lists, smart sorting, and multi-platform sync.

- [Amazing Marvin](https://amazingmarvin.com) – Highly customizable task system with workflows for procrastination control and planning.

- [Sunsama](https://www.sunsama.com) – Daily planner that time-blocks tasks into your calendar for realistic, focused execution.

- [Motion](https://www.usemotion.com) – AI schedules tasks into your calendar automatically to minimize planning overhead.

- [Structured](https://structured.app) – Timeline-based day planner for iOS/macOS focused on routines and visual scheduling.

- [Apple Reminders](https://www.apple.com/ios/reminders) – Native Apple to-dos with lists, smart suggestions, shared tasks, and Siri input.

- [Google Tasks](https://workspace.google.com/products/tasks) – Lightweight tasks integrated into Gmail and Google Calendar for quick capture.

- [Superlist](https://www.superlist.com) – Modern task lists designed for personal and team workflows with clean UI and speed.

### Team Tasking

- [Asana](https://asana.com) – Team work management with tasks, timelines, goals, approvals, and structured workflows.

- [ClickUp](https://clickup.com) – All-in-one tasks/docs/goals with heavy customization, views, automations, and team scale.

- [Monday.com](https://monday.com) – Work OS for teams with boards, automations, dashboards, and project templates.

- [Basecamp](https://basecamp.com) – Simple project communication with message boards, to-dos, docs, and schedule in one place.

- [Teamwork](https://www.teamwork.com) – Client-focused project + task management with time tracking and deliverable workflows.

- [Wrike](https://www.wrike.com) – Enterprise project management with approvals, automation, and robust reporting.

- [Linear](https://linear.app) – Fast issue tracking for product/engineering teams with keyboard-first workflows and tight integrations.

- [Jira](https://www.atlassian.com/software/jira) – Industry-standard issue tracking for agile teams with boards, sprints, and reporting.

- [Trello](https://trello.com) – Kanban boards with cards, checklists, power-ups, and lightweight team collaboration.

## Notes & Knowledge

### PKM & Knowledge Bases

- [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) – Local-first knowledge base with backlinks, graph view, plugins, and Markdown vaults.

- [Evernote](https://evernote.com) – Notes and web clipping with powerful search, notebooks, and cross-device sync.

- [OneNote](https://www.onenote.com) – Microsoft note system with freeform pages, notebooks, and deep Office integration.

- [Roam Research](https://roamresearch.com) – Networked note-taking built around daily notes, links, and graph thinking.

- [Reflect](https://reflect.app) – Personal knowledge base with backlinks, calendar integration, and clean writing experience.

- [Craft](https://www.craft.do) – Beautiful docs and notes with blocks, sharing, and Apple ecosystem polish.

- [Bear](https://bear.app) – Markdown notes with tags, encryption options, and a fast writing-first interface.

- [Mem](https://mem.ai) – AI-assisted notes designed for quick capture and retrieval without rigid folder structure.

- [Capacities](https://capacities.io) – “Object-based” knowledge management to capture people, projects, and ideas as linked entities.

- [Tana](https://tana.inc) – Structured notes with nodes, supertags, and powerful organization for complex knowledge.

### Markdown Notes

- [Joplin](https://joplinapp.org) – Open-source notes with Markdown, encryption, sync options, and web clipper.

- [Standard Notes](https://standardnotes.com) – End-to-end encrypted notes with extensions and long-term privacy-first storage.

- [Zettlr](https://www.zettlr.com) – Markdown editor for writers and researchers with citations and note organization.

- [Typora](https://typora.io) – Minimal Markdown editor with seamless live preview for clean writing workflows.

- [iA Writer](https://ia.net/writer) – Distraction-free writing app with focus mode and Markdown support.

### Spaced Repetition

- [Anki](https://apps.ankiweb.net) – Powerful spaced repetition flashcards with customization and large community decks.

- [AnkiWeb](https://ankiweb.net) – Cloud sync and web access for Anki decks across devices.

- [Quizlet](https://quizlet.com) – Flashcards and learning modes for quick memorization and exam prep workflows.

- [RemNote](https://www.remnote.com) – Notes + spaced repetition combined for study systems and long-term knowledge retention.
